The Doctor is the central character in the long-running British science fiction television series "Doctor Who." The character is a Time Lord, a humanoid alien with the ability to travel through time and space in the TARDIS, a time machine disguised as a British police box. The Doctor is usually depicted as an eccentric, intelligent, and compassionate individual who travels the universe, helping people, righting wrongs, and facing various adversaries, often saving civilizations along the way. The Doctor is known for their ability to regenerate into a new form when mortally wounded, allowing for different actors to portray the character over the show's history.
